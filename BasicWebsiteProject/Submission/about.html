    What's happening in Queenscliffe?



      Get amongst Queenscliffe's food scene

      Guests can try one of the famous burgers at Fergburger, grab a sweet treat from Patagonia Chocolates or Cookie Time Cookie Bar, or choose from one of the many fine-dining restaurants at Queenscliffe.


      Get moving with one of the many walking trails

      We have some of the most scenic short walks and multi-day walks right here in Queenscliffe, whether you are looking for an easy, scenic short walk or a multi-day trail. Find out more here.


      Embark on a golf adventure on one of New Zealand's impressive courses

      Golfing greats are drawn to New Zealand for a variety of reasons, and for good reason. A golfer's paradise, Queenscliffe boasts some of the finest and most unique golf courses in New Zealand. These courses include Jack's Point, Millbrook Resort and The Hills. Within 30 minutes from your location, you will find six golf courses suitable for golfers of all skill levels.


      Check Queenscliffe from above

      There is no better way to experience the natural splendor of Queenscliffe than by taking scenic flights. You can take your vacation to the next level through the use of a light plane or helicopter, which allows you to enjoy lush grasslands, tussocks, vast lakes, and beautiful beaches. Book a helicopter ride that also offer alpine landings to give you more time to take in the views. For those who are feeling a little more daring, try out paragliding or book a hot air balloon ride as the sun rises to a new day.
